How to Connect Your Google Sheet
1
Financials — Auto-LoadedRevenue, COGs, Payroll, and Net Income are pulled automatically from the Aviva Partner Scorecard. Just click Load Data.
2
Goals & NotesUpload aviva_data_template.xlsx to Google Sheets, fill in the Goals tab, publish it as CSV, and paste the URL in the Goals field above.
3
Data SourceFinancials come from partner submissions to the scorecard. Partners who have not yet submitted will show — on their tiles.
4
Refresh AnytimeClick Load Data at any time to pull the latest scorecard submissions and goals together.
